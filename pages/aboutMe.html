About Me

Hi, this is Hania! I am a Content Writer who is passionate about creating content that inspires innovation and resonates with audiences.

My journey began at the University of Toronto, Canada, where I pursued an undergrad in English and participated in extracurriculars such as the university’s creative writing magazine. My designation as the Scribes Marketing Director, as well as my undergrad writing courses, further ignited my interest in the art of compelling writing.

I joined Army Burn Hall College, where I continued my English degree alongside curating and honing my writing skills through freelance article writing.

Over the last year, I have been working as a Technical Content Writer at JMM Technologies, a tech powerhouse and multinational company. During my tenure, I have had the privilege to work with clients across various industries through a wide range of projects.
